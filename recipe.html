Ingredients
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrots
  • 1 beets
  • 3 potatoes
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 bell peppers
  • 1/2 cabbage
  • dill
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • oil
  • salt
Instructions
  • 1. Finely chop the onion, put in a pot of cold water (about 3 liters), salt and boil.
  • 2. Saute the grated beets and carrots in a little oil until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  • 3. Grate the tomatoes and put into boiling water.
  • 4. Then we put beets and carrots in a saucepan, boil.
  • 5. Cut potatoes and bell pepper into cubes and place in a saucepan.
  • 6. Cabbage cut into strips and put in a boiling borscht.
  • 7. Add dill, garlic, salt (if necessary), boil for 5 minutes.
